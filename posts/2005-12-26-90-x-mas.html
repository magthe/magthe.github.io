X-mas

2005-12-26 - Magnus Therning

Christmas eve started out rather busy. Cleaning, some last-minute shopping, that sort of thing. Once the evening arrived and we sat down to eat it really did feel like Christmas. Surrounded by good friends, enjoying good food, and excellent wine. The day ended with exchanging gifts.

Christmas day was equally hectic on the food front. More friends were arriving, this time bringing a few kids, and our hosts ended up being short of chairs. This isn’t much of a problem when Indonesians are concerned, sitting on the floor isn’t looked down on :-) I couldn’t help but escape into Grand Theft Auto on PSP2 after dinner. I truly suck at it, almost two hours playing and I only managed to complete one mission.
