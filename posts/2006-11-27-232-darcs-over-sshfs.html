Darcs over sshfs

2006-11-27 - Magnus Therning

A while ago I found out that darcs doesn’t work over sshfs out of the box. Of course there was a workaround. Yesterday I was at it again, and this time it was sshfs that gave me grief. After another exchange of emails I found a solution.

At the moment this is what’s necessary:

% sshfs -oworkaround=rename <remote path> <local path>
% export DARCS_SLOPPY_LOCKS=1
% darcs put <local path>/<repo name>
