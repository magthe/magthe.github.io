Bye Salmon, hope to see you again, soon!

2007-04-27 - Magnus Therning

I just finished listening to How to Disappear Completely. The last few episodes being almost too gripping, I ended it much too late last night. This is, by far, the best audio book I’ve listened to since I heard Ernst-Hugo Järegård reading H.P. Lovecraft in my early teens.

Go ahead, try the first chapter. I don’t think you’ll regret it!
